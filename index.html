Welcome to 2048 Numberwang, a 2048 variant based on the popular, long-running maths quiz show that’s simply everyone. How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles touch, advanced mathematical calculations determine whether that’s Numberwang!

Original created by Gabriele Cirulli. Based on 1024 by Veewo Studio and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer.
